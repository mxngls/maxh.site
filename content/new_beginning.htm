title: New Beginnings subtitle: What is this place anyway?

It has been something like two and a half years since I first published content online under a domain I owned. Since then, I haven't written much and have spent most of the time iterating on the look and feel of this site. I am still not totally satisfied, but think I've landed on something that I feel more comfortable with. Something that represents certain (aesthetic) values I hold. One of the reasons why I didn't write much during that time is that I disliked the tone a lot of my writing had. Every paragraph felt heavy. I think I failed by trying too hard to establish some false sense of gravitas. Another thing that I was too indecisive about is the actual content and what I want this place to be. The answer is simply "me". In that sense, what I write here will from now on be an honest record of my thoughts. Nothing more or less.
